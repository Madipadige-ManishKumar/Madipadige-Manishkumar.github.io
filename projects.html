AIHealthpro

AIhealpro is an innovative disease prediction platform designed to assess health conditions based on easy, everyday questions. Powered by machine learning algorithms, it analyzes user responses to predict potential illnesses with accuracy and reliability. The goal of AIhealpro is to make healthcare more accessible by providing early insights, empowering individuals to take proactive steps toward their well-being. With a focus on simplicity and user-friendliness, AIhealpro bridges the gap between advanced technology and practical healthcare solutions.

Free Broker

The FreeBroker is a web-based application that connects buyers and sellers of houses. The system allows buyers to search for available properties within a specified pincode and presents relevant properties based on location and price. Additionally, the application features a predictive model that helps determine whether a property is likely to be a "best seller" based on its listed price.

Pdf Summarizer

The PDF Summarizer and Q&A Tool is a web application designed to allow users to interact with PDF documents in an intuitive and insightful way. By uploading a PDF file, users can not only get concise summaries of the content but also ask specific questions related to the document. The system uses a powerful Hugging Face model to process the PDF text and generate relevant answers or summarize the content effectively.

Employee Manager System

The Employee Management System (EMS) is a web application designed to streamline and automate employee work hour tracking, attendance management, and leave requests. Employees can log in at the start of their shift and log out at the end of the day, ensuring accurate work hour records. Additionally, employees can request leave from their respective managers, and both managers and employees can view attendance reports, including remaining leave balances.
